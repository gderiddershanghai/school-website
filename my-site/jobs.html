We’re looking for enthusiastic ESL teachers who want to spark curiosity in young learners and experience the best of Shanghai.

The Position

  • Role: English Second-Language Teacher
  • Schedule: Evenings & weekends (two consecutive weekdays off)
  • Hours: 18 – 25 teaching hrs / week
  • Location: Huangpu District, Shanghai (near Lu Ban Rd Station)
  • Students: Ages 3-12, class size 4-6

Pay & Benefits

  • 2-month paid probation
  • ¥21 k – 26 k RMB net (≈ US $3 k – 3.8 k)
  • Typical overtime ¥1.5 k – 2.5 k (earn ¥30 k+ total)
  • Monthly housing allowance ¥3.5 k
  • All Chinese holidays + extra week at CNY & summer
  • Contract-end bonus ¥5 k – 15 k
  • Annual trip & team-building events
  • Health insurance & full arrival support
  • Ongoing training & pro-development

Requirements

  • Minimum one-year commitment
  • Native English speaker with clean background check
  • Bachelor’s degree (any field)
  • TEFL certificate (completed or in progress)
  • A genuine love of working with kids!
