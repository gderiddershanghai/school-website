How Flying English Took Flight

Flying English is a boutique language school in downtown Shanghai founded in 2015 by two mothers—a former chemical-engineer-turned-entrepreneur and a business consultant—who were disappointed with existing after-school options. Partnering with an education consultant, they set out to raise the bar for young learners.

“I received offers from many schools when I first got to Shanghai, but I’m incredibly happy I chose Flying English. Everyone here truly cares about making a difference. - Tina”

The team in 2018 - After a Laser Tag session.

Milestones

  1. 2014   Doors open with 2 classrooms and 12 students.
  2. 2017   Moved to current Huangpu HQ;
  3. 2019   Passed 500 enrolment; Opened a second campus - Total Staff of 40
  4. 2020   Covid hit - borders closed & closed second campus :(
  5. 2025   Looking for you to join our team!

Our Mission

Remain a boutique centre that delivers high-quality English education to young learners while nurturing creativity, confidence, and curiosity.

What We’ve Achieved

  • Taught 1,000+ young learners since 2015
  • Built a robust ESL & Language-Arts curriculum
  • Ran seasonal STEM and Arts & Crafts camps
  • Survived a global pandemic
  • Fostered a fun, close-knit team that keeps teachers long term

In 2024, we took our team to Bali for a week of fun and bonding.
